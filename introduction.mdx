Shootmail is a template-first mail platform with scheduling and analytics. You can register your domain and send beautiful mails
using pre-made templates directly from code. 

**Templates**: The growing list of templates covers many use cases like authentication emails,
order updates, product announcements, product updates, app launches and many more. These mail templates are well tested on
multiple email clients like apple mail, outlook, gmail etc and support light and dark modes.

**Analytics**: Basic analytics like mail deliveries, opens, clicks and bounces, plus, advanced analytics that gives you
complete visibility on the click trend over a period of time, top performing links, regions and devices.

**Scheduling**: You can schedule your emails for upto 1 year in future and cancel anytime before they are send,
right from the [SDK](https://www.npmjs.com/package/shootmail).
