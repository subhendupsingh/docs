Shootmail has pre-designed, good looking and well tested email templates that you can call from your code, pass in
configuration and send using your favourite email service like resend, postmark, sendgrid etc. All the mails are well tested on
multiple email clients like apple mail, outlook, gmail etc and support light and dark modes.
